Papers Primary focus - Numerical study of dam-break problem for important families of gSGN equations (improved dispersion, serre to swwe, regularised shallow water).
\begin{itemize}
	\item Discussion of different implementations and effect of reconstruction (particularly of derivatives) on solutions
	\item First robust numerical solutions for the family of equations for steep gradient problem
	\item Robustness of method in presence of weak discontinuity (spike in G)
	\item Number of different implementations of methods demonstrating same behaviour (justifying our observations)
\end{itemize}
